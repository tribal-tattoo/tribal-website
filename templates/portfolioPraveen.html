Praveen Gowda

Tattoo Designer

“Allow your passion to become your purpose, and it will one day become your profession.” Pravin ,he is a living example of this. This passionate artist hails from the lovely town of Kushalnagar, and has had experience in various fields including sales and marketing. Art has always been a part of his life, ever since childhood. Be it model making or painting, he has always been inclined towards creating. Artistic explorations led become a self-taught tattoo artist, in the year 2011. With further gain of experience from freelancing, he founded Tribal Tattoo Mysore in the year 2013. He has never looked back since. He attended his first convention in 2016. The inspiration he received there pushed him to achieve so much more in the following years. Here is a list of awards won by him so far:

  • Best Neo Traditional Tattoo
    • Winner- Inksoul tattoo convention, Bangalore-2018
    • Third place- Goa Tattoo Festival, Goa- 2019

  • Big Black and Grey Tattoo
    • Winner- Inksoul tattoo convention, Pune-2018
    • Winner- Mystical Sri Lanka Tattoo Festival, Sri Lanka-2018

  • Best Realism Tattoo
    • Winner- Heartwork tattoo convention, New Delhi-2018
    • Third Place- Heartwork tattoo convention, New Delhi-2019

  • Best Small Color Tattoo
    • Third Place- Heartwork tattoo convention, New Delhi-2019

  • Best Collaboration Tattoo

  • Best comic character tattoo
