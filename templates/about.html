What We Do

Hi! We are a group of fun loving artists who love to explore. We strongly believe that tattoos are so much more than mere ink on skin. Every tattoo has a story to tell, be it of an experience or an emotion. It’s the most personalized form of expression of one’s individuality and we try to do justice to the art form by customizing your tattoos for your unique requirements. Established in 2013 by our founder Mr. Pravin Gowda, Tribal Tattoo Mysore was one of the earliest tattoo studios in Mysuru. Over the years, we’ve grown into a team of artists who strive to excel in the craft, explore the art form and fully satisfy our clients. Today, Tribal Tattoo Mysore is not only one of the best in Karnataka but has also made its name in the country. Tribal Tattoo Mysore is not just about tattoos, mind you! We believe that knowing and remembering one’s roots is the key to grow and excel as an individual. Driven by art, we also make sure to keep creating anything and everything artistic, from pencil sketches, charcoal paintings, to acrylic paintings and even handicrafts. Exploration is our mantra and everything we see around us is an opportunity to create. So hit us up with your ideas, sit back and be prepared to be amazed!
