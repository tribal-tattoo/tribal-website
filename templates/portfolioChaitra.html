Chaitra Umamaheshwar

Tattoo Designer

Born and brought up in the coastal city of Mangalore, she has graduated from BMSCE, Bangalore with Bachelors in Architecture. During her time in college, her interest in art and design has led her to be proficient at charcoal and acrylic painting techniques alongside sketching and digital skills. She has always been explorative with regards to various media of art and strongly believes that art in any form reflects individuality. She was trained in tattooing by Purplemonk Tattooz, Bangalore, where she continued to work as a tattoo artist for a year. She has been tattooing since 2019 and her designs are mostly based on her experiences with graphic design, painting styles and abstraction.

Interests: Abstract, Watercolour, Dotwork, Realism, Portraits.
